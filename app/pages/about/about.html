Company Profile
Rashi E Waste is a niche E Waste Management Solution Provider and IT services specializing in delivering innovative solutions & services to leading organizations. Armed with able, experienced professionals we help global companies gain significant market advantage through customer-centric, high-quality and cost-effective hardware and IT solutions.
Our team comprises of highly motivated, competent and passionate people with hands-on experience in a spectrum of technology and business domains. The people strengths, supported by a robust and scalable global delivery model, help us meet our customer expectations and project deadlines in the most efficient manner
We at Rashi E Waste trade out many discarded machines which contain usable parts which could be salvaged and combined with other used equipment to create a working unit. NGOs and manufacturers would need help in environmentally sound recycling of e-waste which requires sophisticated technology and processes, which are not only very expensive, but also need specific skills and training for the operation.
We have about 30+ years of total experience in all of the IT processes and the respective controls that are required for such operations, including a massive learning and working experience from the Big Blue IBM. Currently we are in the process of developing guidelines for environmentally sound recycling of E Wastes and which is our final goal and vision.
The best option for dealing with E wastes is to reduce the volume. We as designers are trying to ensure that the product is built for re-use, repair and/or upgradeability. Stress is being laid on use of less toxic, easily recoverable and recyclable materials which can be taken back for refurbishment, remanufacturing, disassembly and reuse. Recycling and reuse of material are the next level of potential options to reduce e-waste Hence in the beginning we have drawn a boundary for ourselves to start of with at least 2 or 3 steps of this entire lifecycle and to act as a Refurbisher and to put these things into proper use and also to build a better world.
